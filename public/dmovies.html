The Best Movies According to David

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Alita: Battle Angel

My most rewatched among the Top Three. I enjoy this movie. I get goosebumps everytime I see the protagonist awaken to power.

Old Boy

This movie sucked me in the first time. I could not stop thinking about how twisted everything turned out. It can feel humbling when you put yourself in the shoes of the victim.

Jackie Chan's First Strike

One of my childhood's favorite movies. I had it on VHS and watched it more than once. The action and comedy were so new and fascinating to me as a kid.
